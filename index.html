FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

Our AI is powered by GPT-4o Vision, one of the most advanced image understanding models in the world. It analyzes lighting quality, color harmony, furniture placement, clutter, decor personality, and style consistency — the same things a professional interior designer would notice. It references specific objects it can actually see in your photo, not generic advice. Results vary by photo quality, but the more natural light and detail in your image, the more accurate and specific the feedback.
Never. Your photo is sent directly to the AI for analysis and immediately discarded — it is not saved to any database, shared with third parties, or used to train any model. We don't log the image on our servers at all. Your home is private and we take that seriously. You can verify this in our privacy policy below.
Every visitor gets 2 free analyses per day — no account, no email, no credit card required. This resets every 24 hours. If you want to analyze multiple rooms, track your progress over time, or unlock the full style report, upgrade to Pro for unlimited daily analyses and the complete feature set.
RoomGlow works on any indoor space — bedrooms, living rooms, desk setups, closets, kitchens, and bathrooms. The best photos are well-lit, taken from a corner or doorway to show as much of the room as possible, and in landscape orientation. Avoid very dark photos or shots with heavy filters applied, as these reduce the AI's ability to accurately read colors and lighting.
After analyzing your room, the AI recommends specific product types that would improve your score — like a warm floor lamp for a room with harsh overhead lighting, or a woven rug to add texture to a bare floor. Each tip links directly to an Amazon search for that exact product type so you can browse real options instantly. RoomGlow earns a small commission on purchases at no extra cost to you — this is what keeps the free tier free.
Progress tracking is coming in our next update (v1.3.0) — it will let you re-analyze the same room after making changes and watch your score improve over time. Pro users currently get unlimited analyses, all 5 tips unlocked, the full style report, and the what's working / what needs work breakdown — all for $7.99/month, cancel anytime.
